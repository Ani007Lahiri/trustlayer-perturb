TrustLayer

A calibrated-trust layer for perturbation-biology prediction — "a pLDDT for perturbation biology."

Foundation models don't reliably beat linear baselines at predicting perturbation effects (Ahlmann-Eltze, Huber & Anders, Nat Methods 2025). So the bottleneck isn't accuracy — it's knowing which prediction to trust before spending scarce wet-lab budget. TrustLayer wraps any predictor with split-conformal calibration and a default-deny commit gate. Everything below is pre-registered, hash-verified, and self-red-teamed.
