The un-copyable asset

Everyone ships false confidence. We ship calibration.

  • Selective prediction: abstain on the bottom, and error falls as coverage drops.
  • De-confounded: we regress out expression, power & essentiality so "trust" isn't just "highly expressed."
  • Grounded in theory: omnigenic core vs peripheral — Pritchard's own model.
How we don't get embarrassed
